Built to Help Service Businesses Grow

AI Peak Biz was founded on a simple observation: service businesses lose thousands of dollars every month to missed calls, no-shows, and weak online reviews. The technology to fix all three exists. Most small businesses do not have the time or expertise to set it up. We do it for them.

We are an AI automation agency based in Kingman, Arizona. We build and manage AI chatbots, voice assistants, and reputation management systems for service businesses across the country. Our clients include chiropractors, dentists, med spas, law firms, home service companies, and more.

Every system we build is configured for the specific business it serves. We train the AI on your services, your scheduling rules, your FAQs, and your brand voice. We connect it to your calendar, your CRM, and your review platforms. Then we manage it ongoing so you focus on what you do best.

This is not a DIY platform. This is not a tool you have to figure out. This is a done-for-you service. You tell us about your business. We build the system. You approve it. We turn it on. Your AI starts working.
